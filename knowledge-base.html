Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with QiAlly services?

+

Getting started is easy! Simply contact me through the contact form or email me directly. I'll schedule a consultation to discuss your needs and create a tailored plan for your project.

What services do you offer?

+

I offer three main services: Strategic Scoping to define project boundaries and requirements, Voting Systems to implement effective decision-making processes, and Strategic Guidance to provide expert advice and direction.

How do I access the client portal?

+

Once you become a client, you'll receive access credentials for the client portal. You can access it at portal.qially.com/client. If you need help with access, please contact me directly.

What is your response time for support?

+

I typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please use the contact form or email me directly at support@qially.com.
